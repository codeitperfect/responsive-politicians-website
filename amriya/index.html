About Me

Wakili Mtetezi

I’m a Lawyer and an Advocate of the High Court who is passionate about using quality leadership skills and empathy to solve real-world problems that are facing the residents of Mjambere Constituency and its environs.

During my time as a Law student at the University of Nairobi and my tenure as a Nominated MCA at the County Government of Mombasa, I have struggled to summarize my credentials and experiences using the traditional cliche of “Amriya has over X years of experience in…” simply because I have passionately spent most of my time in creating an equitable society and fighting for human rights.

My motivation to vie for this noble seat has been driven by change, a fundamental political change that will make a difference, improve the living standards, foster development and leave a forever lasting legacy. My passion to serve the people with harmony and work for their well being made me to come up with four key agendas, agendas that have immense opportunity to bring change once accorded the attention they deserve and pushed with bona fide faith.

Together let’s delve further into the world of quality leadership by casting your vote on Amriya Boy Juma come August 09th, 2022 general election.

My Agendas

Pamoja Tuokoe Mjambere

Equal
Representation

Under my leadership, every person will be subjected to a fair, credible and equal representation. I will be the frontliner in chantting and promoting the toolkits that are essential in ensuring that there is a proportional representation in the Mjambere Constituency and all over across Mombasa county. Gender diversity will be one of the key areas that I will focus on in this blueprint.

Infrastructural
Enhancment

Infrastructure forms the building block of any developed area, I have a mega plan that will be executed with the sole purpose of transforming Mjambere edifices. Every chance in strengthening the infrastructure comes with so many employment opportunities that will be unbiasedly rolled out to the residents of Mjambere.

Empowernment of the Youths,
Women and PWD's

Youth, women and people living with disability have been sidelined for quite a litany of time, my leadership will mark an end to these atrocities. I will ensure that they are represented in the social-political and economic spheres. The blueprints of delivering this initiative cuts across different aspects that include but not limited to; employment opportunities, vocational trainings and skills enhancement.

Suistainable
Development

The ability to cater for the human development goals requires a profound culture of utilizing the natural resources whilst upholding the rich ecosystem. Through this agenda, I have a top notch plan that has been crafted with so much love and sparkle to end poverty, protect our environment and strengthen peaceful co-existence amongst the people of Mjambere.
